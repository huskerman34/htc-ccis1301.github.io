Weekly Assignments & Participation

Weight: 20%
One or more small assignments which are due weekly. These may include one or more of the following: a discussion topic, a short written assignment, a simple web page, and/or a short quiz. These assignments are a measure of your participation in the course for that week and are not accepted late.

Exams

Weight: 20%
There will be four exams spread throughout the semester. Each exam will be cumulative, meaning it will cover all of the material from the course to date. Since each exam will progressively cover more material, the later exams will contribute more points to your overall exam grade.

Case Study Project

Weight: 30%
We will be simulating the development a website for a small business called Pacific Trails Resort. The development of this is project is laid out chapter by chapter in your book. The assignment submissions are grouped into multi-chapter chunks, so make sure to pay attention to which chapters are due for each part.

Final Portfolio Project

Weight: 30%
Development of your personal online resume and portfolio using Bootstrap. Bootstrap is a framework of CSS and JavaScript components for the development of a responsive website, and it is widely used for the development of professional websites.
